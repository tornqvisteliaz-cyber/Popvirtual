ATC Academy

Controller Training Pipeline

Progress through a five-stage academy with mentor pairing, live session debriefs, SOP drills, and event certifications.

Stage 1 · Fundamentals

Phraseology, strip marking, radar etiquette, and sector awareness.

Stage 2 · Tower

Ground flow, runway coordination, VFR handling, and departure sequencing.

Stage 3 · Approach

STARs, vectoring, transition planning, missed approach handling, and workload management.

Stage 4 · Center

Enroute conflicts, oceanic procedures, event staffing, and sector handoffs.

Stage 5 · Instructor

Check rides, grading, team leadership, and academy development.
